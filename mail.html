This is a note to help interpret this chart: the chart is sorted on the column titled “% to Total” in the heading “Summary of 31+ Past Due”.
This column shows the dollar value of the “31+ days” invoice amounts as a percentage of the Total dollar amount sold.
This does not consider the terms that might apply to various customers. It’s based on the invoice date, and just 31 or more days after this date.
If one location has a high number of accounts, or large dollar sales, that are given extended terms (more than 30 days), the dollar value of “31+ days” may represent a high percentage of the Total value sold.
Very often the Major category is near the top for these reasons – National accounts likely with extended terms.
Large pre-paid amounts can work in an opposite way.
